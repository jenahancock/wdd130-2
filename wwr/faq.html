Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need previous rafting experience?

No. Most of our trips are beginner-friendly and guided by experienced river guides. We go over safety, paddling, and what to expect before we get on the water.

What should I wear on the river?

Wear quick-dry clothing (no cotton if possible), secure sandals or water shoes, and bring dry clothes for after the trip. We provide helmets and life jackets.

Is there an age or weight limit?

Most family trips allow kids 8+ but it can vary depending on river conditions. Contact us for specifics before booking.

What’s included in the price?

Your guided trip, rafting gear (PFD/life jacket, helmet, paddles), and safety instruction are all included. Some trips also include snacks or lunch, depending on the package.

What if the weather is bad?

We run trips in light rain — you’re getting wet anyway! In unsafe conditions (lightning, flooding, etc.), we’ll reschedule or issue a credit.

How do I book a trip?

You can book through our Contact Us page or call us directly. We’ll confirm your date, group size, and trip details.
